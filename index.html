Hou Yifan

World's number one woman chess player.

Hou Yifan (Chinese: 侯逸凡; pinyin: Hóu Yìfán pronunciation) is a chess grandmaster and former chess prodigy. She has been the Women's World Chess Champion three times, the youngest ever to win the title, as well as the youngest female player ever to qualify for the title of Grandmaster.
